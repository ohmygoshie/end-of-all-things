The End of All Things

Now, I know what you're wondering. What is this? Why a website? Well, I have answers. This is a suicide note. It's a website because it's easier to access and cooler in my opinion. Even when I'm dying, I have to be cool I guess. Honestly, I don't even know where to start. I just want to die, simply put. My life has been falling apart for a year and a half now and I cant take it anymore. I don't feel like straight up explaining why I want to die, so this is going to be more of a goodbye note to everyone who is or was in my life that I believe was important (excluding my family).
